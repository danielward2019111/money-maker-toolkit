Example Executive Summary

The biggest likely revenue leak is not traffic. It is the gap between a buyer showing intent and the business responding with a clear, fast, confidence-building next step. The fastest fixes are confirmation messaging, follow-up sequencing, stronger proof placement, and a clearer handoff from enquiry to booking.

Top 5 Example Revenue Leaks

Rank Leak Why it matters Fast fix
1 No immediate confirmation after enquiry Buyers may not know what happens next and can keep shopping. Add a confirmation email and page with next step, timing, and proof.
2 Follow-up relies on manual memory Interested leads can go cold before anyone notices. Add a 3-message follow-up sequence over 72 hours.
3 Proof is too far from the CTA The buyer is asked to act before trust is built. Move case studies, reviews, or numbers close to the main CTA.
4 Form asks too little qualifying information Sales calls start with missing context. Add 2-4 qualifying questions that improve routing and preparation.
5 No recovery path for abandoned booking Warm buyers who hesitate are lost. Send a useful reminder with proof, objection handling, and a booking link.
